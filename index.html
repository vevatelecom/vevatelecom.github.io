Mission

VEVA is building a platform to fund regional WISPS via equity-like structures. Wireless networks in most countries have become concentrated, which keeps competition at bay despite more powerful & efficient network equipment. The reason is simple: regional operators are limited by access to capital. Ultra-fast, low-latency wireless networks are critical for this next phase of the digital revolution. We’re bringing a decentralized funding model that supports regional operators. We aim to drive superior returns while bringing leading edge performance, competition and coverage.
